Blaster

Everyone who enjoys transformers will undoubtedly know the Decepticon Soundwave. However, I've always tended toward the Autobots. Fortunately, they have their own version in for the form of Blaster!

As though created to be the exact opposite, Blaster posseses significantly more charisma and is frequently talking. Going so far as to even be a radio personality in some continuities.

Similarly to Shockwave, Blaster houses some Mini-Cassette Autobots within his chest cavity. Some of those include:

  • Ramhorn
  • Steeljaw
  • Rewind
  • Eject

Like all transformers, he has had a slew of alternate formes:

  1. Cassette Player
  2. Cybertronian Sonic Tank
  3. Cybertronian Stealth Bomber
  4. Satelite Laptop
